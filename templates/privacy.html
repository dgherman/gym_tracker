Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ last_updated }}

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in to Gym Tracker using Google OAuth, we collect:

  • Basic profile information: Your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account
  • Google account ID: A unique identifier for your Google account
  • Gym session data: Information about your training sessions, including duration, trainer, and timestamps
  • Package purchases: Records of session packages you've purchased

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide access to your account
  • Track your gym sessions and remaining session packages
  • Display personalized dashboards and reports
  • Provide trainer selection and session management features

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database and is:

  • Protected by industry-standard security measures
  • Only accessible to you and authorized administrators
  • Not shared with any third parties
  • Not used for marketing or advertising purposes

4. Google OAuth

We use Google OAuth for authentication. When you sign in:

  • You are redirected to Google's secure login page
  • We receive basic profile information from Google after you authorize our app
  • We do not have access to your Google password
  • You can revoke Gym Tracker's access at any time through your Google Account permissions

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your session history and data
  • Revoke access to your Google account information

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account, please contact the administrator.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the application.
